Paintings

Real-life, paint-on-canvas kind

I paint landscapes and scenes inspired by nature, memory, and imagined places. Trees, paths, water, old buildings, soft skies — I like settings that feel peaceful but not empty, as if someone just stepped out of frame.

Most of my work is done in acrylic on canvas, with an emphasis on color, light, and mood over realism.

Things I tend to paint:

  • ✦ Landscapes and natural spaces
  • ✦ Gentle light, seasons, and weather
  • ✦ Places that feel familiar even if you’ve never been there

About Me

I like working across different mediums because they scratch different creative itches. Painting lets me slow down and focus on mood and color. Games let me build systems, tell stories, and create spaces people can interact with.

No matter the format, I’m usually trying to make something that feels thoughtful, intentional, and a little comforting — even when it’s weird.
